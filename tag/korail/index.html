Korail Timetables

Researching a trip to Korea I found it difficult to use the online tools to plan trips, especially when there are connections involved. So far I have not found a tool that works as well as the sadly lobotomised Hyperdia used to work for Japan, or even as well as the combination of JapanTravel and JapanTransit that I use today does.
