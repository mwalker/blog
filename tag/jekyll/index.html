Transitioning from prose.sh to jekyll

While I liked prose.sh as a blog provider I have found that the workflow doesn’t quite suit me. I would prefer something less magical and a bit more boring. As the basis of Github Pages and written in ruby, a language I keep coming back to, I decided that jekyll qualified for me.
